The Dream Center of Jackson

Welcome

Welcome to The Dream Center Of Jackson! Here, house families and individuals who have experienced crisis in their lives such as; loss of employment, housing or other life-controlling problems that rendered them homeless.

What We Do

We are a lighthouse and a beacon of hope to all women and women with children who have a strong desire to change their way of life by implementing Biblical principles so that the cycle of chronic homelessness shall be broken.
