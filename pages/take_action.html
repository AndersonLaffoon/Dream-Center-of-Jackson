We Bless Your Consideration!

Want to help? Learn more about how you can help the women and children here at The Dream Center

Why Help?

The Dream Center needs your help. You could be a big help to us. Your help could improve someone's life. A small contribution can have big effects. Helping someone in need can give you a great feeling of satisfaction. It is our duty to look out for people in our community and to assist them in any way possible.

Ways To Help

Volunteer

Want to volunteer today? You won’t find a more rewarding way to help others.

Food Donations

Donate food to the Dream Center. We ask for fresh fruits, vegetables, bread, milk, meat , canned foods are greatly needed.

Prayers

A simple prayer can go a long way. Please pray that God will sustain us through this homeless ministry for women and children.

Skilled Labor

We need help to keep the building in good condition. Electricians, plumbers, dry wall experts… all are needed to help with building upkeep.

Clothing Donations

Donate clothing to those in need at the Dream Center. New and gently used clothes help build self esteem in our women and children.

Adopt-A-Room

We need rooms. $12,000 builds one family room in our new facility, making it possible to house more individuals in comfort.

A New Building

In November 2016 the building we were in was sold. We are now in temporary spaces. God has blessed us with property and building plans! We need your help. We are asking 3000 churches, civic groups, community groups, and individuals to prayerfully consider a donation of $1000 towards our new facility. For more information about our Capital Campaign call Stephanie Laffoon 731-267-9016.

The Dream Center is a 501C3 Agency. Tax ID#CO10135

Donate online

We use PayPal to accept online donations. PayPal is one of the largest companies that securely facilitates online giving for many different causes. Click the button above to go to PayPal to donate. Of course, we are happy to meet you in person if you want to discuss specifics. Just give us a call at (731) 512-0095.

The Dream Center of Jackson is committed to respecting the privacy of our donors. We have developed this privacy policy to inform you of the way donor information is used and to reassure you that donor information will not be shared with any third party.

The Dream Center will use contact information of donors for the following purposes only:

  • Distribute receipts for donations
  • Send thank you notes to donors
  • Inform donors about upcoming fundraising activities and events
The Dream Center will not sell, rent or lease your personal information to other organizations. We assure you that the identity of all our donors will be kept confidential. It is not our desire to send unwanted mail to our donors. Please contact us if you wish to be removed from our mailing list.

If you have comments or questions about our donor privacy policy, please send us and e-mail at info@dreamcenterjackson.com or call us at 731-512-0095.
